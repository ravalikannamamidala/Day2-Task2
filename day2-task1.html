Favourite Places

Kedaranath
One of the most interesting features of the Kedarnath Temple is its unique lingam. Devotees believe Lord Shiva himself worship himself here. Unlike other lingams that are made of stone or metal, people believe it to be a natural rock formation in the shape of a lingam.
Agra
Agra is a city on the banks of the Yamuna river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh
Telangana
'South of North and North of South,' Telangana State has long been a meeting place for diverse languages and cultures. It is easily the best example for India's composite culture, pluralism and inclusiveness. Located on the uplands of Deccan plateau, Telangana is the link between the North and South of India.
